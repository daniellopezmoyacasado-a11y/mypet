# mypet
Small Project to create a Custom Tamagotchi game
## How to take care of your pet

After you name your pet, you will have to keep it fed and entertained.
To do so, you can feed it when hungry, and or play with it.

Playing too much will cause your pet to get hungry again.

Your pet might also relieve itself from time to time. And they might do it anywhere so be sure to keep an eye out to clean up your screen ;P
